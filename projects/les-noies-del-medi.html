Structuring Content & Crafting Experience

For this case study, the focus is on the homepage, as it best represents the main UX, content-structuring, and storytelling decisions made throughout the project.

The homepage redesign focused on organizing content clearly and creating an engaging user experience. Along with the LNDM team, we reviewed the initial concepts and the brand's mission, vision, and values to define a clear direction. Navigation was simplified, related content was grouped, and wireframes were designed for scannable blocks.

In the sections below, a before-and-after comparison shows how navigation, content structure, and CTA placement were refined to enhance clarity and engagement.
