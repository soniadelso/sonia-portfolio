Research & Information Architecture

Through the review of portfolios from graphic designers, design studios, and awarded websites, I identified best practices, current trends, and opportunities for differentiation. These insights guided the definition of the information architecture, structuring the content according to hierarchy, user flow, and core goals such as showcasing projects, my profile, and facilitating contact.

The final structure enables intuitive navigation from Home to Work, About Me, and Contact, with projects organized by category and accessed through a clear, progressive flow.

Branding & Visual Design

I focused on crafting a clean and consistent interface that reflects my personal design style. I explored color palettes, typography, and UI components to build a cohesive visual identity.

Design Foundations

I developed a cohesive visual system by defining typography and color variables, spacing and grid rules, and reusable UI components such as buttons and navigation. This ensured consistency across the interface.

Interactive Prototyping

I developed a clickable prototype in Figma to test the user experience, navigation, and key flows such as project browsing and contact access.
