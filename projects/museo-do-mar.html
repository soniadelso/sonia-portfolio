Museo do Mar: New Identity

This rebranding project focused on Museo do Mar in Galicia, with the aim of engaging a younger audience while preserving the museum's cultural identity and heritage.

We redefined the visual identity and communication system to create a more approachable and contemporary brand experience, strengthening the museum's connection with new generations of visitors.

Typography & Color

The color palette evokes the sea and Galicia's coastal environment, enhancing brand recognition and visual cohesion across all materials. Typography was carefully selected to balance readability with personality, reflecting the museum's maritime theme and contemporary approach.

Visual Identity

The rebranding focused on creating a fresh and modern visual identity for the museum. The new logo, inspired by the shape of the museum building, connects the identity directly to the architecture. The graphic elements were designed to appeal to a younger audience while preserving the museum's cultural heritage.

Communication Materials

The project included the redesign of marketing and promotional materials, such as posters, flyers, merchandising items and social media graphics. Each piece was crafted to engage a younger audience and ensure consistent brand application across physical and digital touchpoints.
