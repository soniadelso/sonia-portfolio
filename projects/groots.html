From Motion to Point of Sale

The work includes the creation of a corporate video, fully produced, recorded, and edited by me using After Effects, as well as the design of in-store marketing materials such as posters and informational wobblers displayed in supermarkets. All pieces followed Groots' established color palette and typography.

Brand Application

All materials were developed following Groots' established visual identity, applying brand colors, typography, and tone consistently across formats. The project translates the brand into both motion and physical spaces, ensuring coherence and recognition.

Motion Design

The corporate video was conceived as a dynamic and engaging piece, designed to communicate Groots' message in a fresh and youthful way. I was responsible for the full process, including filming, production, and motion design, with editing and animation developed in After Effects.

In-Store Materials

The project includes the design of in-store marketing materials such as posters and informational wobblers displayed in supermarkets. These pieces aim to clearly communicate product features and company values, adapting the message to a commercial and customer-facing environment.
