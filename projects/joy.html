Flyer Design & Visual Communication

The project involved the creation of promotional flyers for the premiere of the theater play JOY. I selected colors, imagery, and typography to reflect the story's narrative, highlighting the critique of consumer society and the protagonist's obsession and pursuit of revenge.

The design aimed to capture the play's dramatic tone and engage the audience, translating the themes into a visually compelling piece.

Visual Design

The flyer's palette was chosen to reflect the dramatic tone of the play, using black and red in accordance with the client's branding. High-quality imagery was selected to complement the palette and reinforce the story's emotional impact. Typography was carefully selected to communicate the play's themes and mood.

Production

The flyer was produced as a print-ready piece, ensuring color accuracy and quality across all materials. Every design decision, from composition to imagery, was aligned with the narrative and branding goals.
